About us

We've been in the business since the D-day when we had to supply bikes for Nazis to enable their escape - 69 years of experience.

Our company provides only the best bikes and the best customer service. Our experts will help you choose the perfect bike for you and at the same time make sure it is the most expensive one.

Bike rental is all about trust so we do not give out helmets. We TRUST that you are capable enough not to get yourself killed.

We also know you value freedom but we value our bikes more so each bike is equipped with state-of-the-art GPS technology. It is there to track your ass and make sure you are not smuggling drugs over the border using our bikes.
